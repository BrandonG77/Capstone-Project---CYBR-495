# 11 - Credential Harvesting

---

## Detection Alert

Based on the Detection Engineer's Splunk and Wireshark findings, it's evident that a credential harvesting attack took place. Activities were logged related to lateral movement, access of sensitive files, registry dumping, secret dumping, memory access, PowerShell usage, and high privilege access to systems.

## Investigation

During investigation, signs such as unusual login activity from unknown locations or IP addresses, consistent MFA requests, a spike in password resets, suspicious emails, and other unauthorized changes to systems are evident of a credential harvesting attack.

## Containment

To mitigate the chances of future credential harvesting attacks, implement phishing-resistant MFA, enforce strict access controls, stay up-to-date with email security, monitor systems for anomalous behavior, use password authentication, and conduct security training for all stakeholders--internal and external.
